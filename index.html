Kawaihae, Hawaii

Hawaii is the 50th state of the United States, formally the Kingdom of Hawaii until the overthrow by United States citizens in the late 1800s. It is formed of 8 major islands, Hawaii Island being the largest, and Oahu being the most populous. Oahu is where the capital, Honolulu, is located. Kawaihae is on Hawaii Island, sometimes called the Big Island as it is geographically larger than all the islands combined.

Although now part of the United States, There are many who wish to break away and form its own Sovereign Kingdom once more. If you visit the islands, those who support Hawaiian Sovereignty have the flag of the state upside down.
